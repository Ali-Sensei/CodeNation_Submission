Purpose of IT in Modern Business

The importance of I.T. on modern business cannot be overstated. Whether a business is run by an individual or is a multinational corporation, the benefits that technology brings allows businesses to open communication with customers, better efficiency, improve teamwork and use resources with ease.



Communication

Customers demand ease-of-use and access to information. As a website that is run 24-7, customers are able to use the website to access the information they need to answer questions that they have, thus allowing the customer to feel more comfortable with the business and feeling safe in the knowledge that their questions are being answered. Due to e-commerce technologies, customers are able to pay for and get the items they need from the comfort of their location anywhere in the world. Technology helps a business be more approachable for customers.

Efficiency

A well-designed website allows a customer to browse through a business’s products and services at their own leisure, saving on staff costs in the long run. Also, due an online presence, money can be saved from purchasing physical space and put more towards getting the product into the customer’s hands. As mentioned above, the convenience of e-commerce in getting things to order from the comfort of the home is highly valued and is more comfortable for the customer. Even running meetings in the business or outside of business is made far easier by having them over the internet.



Teamwork

A business is only made stronger by having members of a team work together, and the better they work together, the better they perform. Technology allows members of a team to stay connected with each other, and the use of an online shared space is priceless in order to relay and access information. For example, the online platform ‘Trello’ can be used as an interactive whiteboard, allowing team members to post topics and to-do lists up on a shareable board, as well as marking off tasks that are completed in real-time. Google has a shareable space called ‘Google Drive’ where companies can sue the shared space to share files and information between each other near-instantaneously, and even work on documents at the same time in collaboration with other team members.

Resources

The fact that the resources of the world are available with a few clicks away allows a business to complete its research in a much shorter time that if that technology was not available. The internet itself is a network of computers all over the world, and there are so many resources that are available to answer questions and solve problems. The fact of the matter is a lot of problems that we have have most likely been solved, and it takes a few minutes on the internet to research and find the solution, and even if the problem isn’t easily solvable, once the solution has been found through independent means, the solution can be shared. This allows businesses from all over the world to help each other.
